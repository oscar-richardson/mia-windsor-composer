Mia Windsor is a composer and producer based in Leeds, UK. After writing a variety of acoustic and electronic pieces, Mia has come to specialise in using technology to expand human performance. She tends to use generative systems, alternative tuning systems and real-time electronic manipulation to do so.

Mia is also an experimental pop artist under the name ‘Pie is Fly’, and focuses on writing music that transitions between the seriousness of contemporary experimental music and the perceived carelessness of the slop pop DIY genre in coherent yet fun ways.
