IN//EX

IN//EX was my first collaboration with Pussy Stamina and was performed at Hatch 2019 at Hyde Park Book Club. The piece explored introversion and extroversion and how it can define our identity. It was performed with a synthesizer, sampler, looped vocals, spoken word and electric guitar.
