'The Birds': Discovery of a Bird Attack

When watching Alfred Hitchcock’s The Birds for the first time, I was disappointed that the majority of scenes have no score, or had synthesized bird sounds at the very most. I decided to score this scene taking influence from Bernard Herrmann scores (notably Psycho), using strings and the occasional synthesizer.
