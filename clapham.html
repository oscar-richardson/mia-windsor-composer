Clapham Junction

I wrote Clapham Junction to accompany a short piece of writing that I wrote at Moniak Mhor Creative Writing Centre in 2017. The piece uses samples of train spotting videos (taken from Smithy’s Transport Page on Facebook) as an attempt to fuse the sounds of a train station with an electronic soundscape.
