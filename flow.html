Flow

This piece is based around two cycles of a yoga sun-salutation variation. The individual instruments represent different parts of the body during the cycle. After running through the cycle multiple times, I made notes on what parts of my body were being moved, stretched and relied on for strength at different points. This then allowed me to construct the piece.
